Creative Avatar Generator API User Manual

Creative Avatar Generator API, a tool that allows you to generate unique avatars based on a seed string of your choice. This manual will guide you through the steps to create avatars in both SVG and PNG formats.

Generating an Avatar

To generate a custom avatar, follow these steps:

Visit the "Getting Started" page on the API.

Fill out the "Enter Seed" field with your chosen seed string.

Select the desired format (SVG or PNG) from the drop-down menu.

Click the "Generate Avatar" button.

The Creative Avatar Generator API will generate and display your avatar.

Get started by visiting the Getting Started page.

Enjoy creating your custom avatars!
